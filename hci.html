User Requirements

Must Have
  • Target at current residents in Camden or prospective residents who are looking to move into Camden
  • Users group: Adults over 18
  • Implemented as a chatbot interface on the Camden Council webpage
Should Have
  • Include the use of buttons for easy response from user
  • Include a spell checker to allow the chatbot to understand what the user means if there is a typo in the question
  • Provide suggestions using keywords if the chatbot does not fully understand the question
  • Provide a reply in simple plain English for easy understanding
  • Allow the chatbot to be used on mobile browser
Could have
  • Include the chatbot in the Camden Council TTS service to improve accessibility
  • Store questions that the chatbot is unable to answer for analysis


Personas

Janice Jones




Motivation & Goals
  • Support her children
  • Help the local community and be involved in charitable events


Janice is a doctor and the mother of two children. She has been living in Camden for 3 years and both of her children are students working very hard on their A-Level exam. Recently, there has been a lot noise coming from her neighbour at night, which makes her children cannot concentrate on revising and did not have enough sleep at night. Due to the consistent noise coming from her neighbours, Janice and her children has been experiencing insomnia and mental strain. Janice feels the situation has gone worse than before and will definitely have an impact on their daily lives. However, she does not know what can she do about it or who to reach out for. Since she is very busy at work during the day, she does not have time to visit the council or calling them as the Camden Council office hours clash with her own working hours. She is very worry about this condition and hoping to find a way to stop this situation from continuing.



Alan Walker




Motivation & Goals
  • Find a new apartment in a great neighbourhood
  • Try to do the good decisions based on as much information as possible


Alan is a 27 year old banker and has been working in the finance sector for 3 years now. He is currently living in Victoria and wishes to move in to Camden as he is working at a bank in Camden. He is looking for buying an apartment in Camden. He sees buying an apartment as an investment, which means the future development of this area should be considered in order to know whether there is a potential of value increase in the future. Therefore, Alan is interested in knowing the future planning of Camden, for example, the infrastructure to be built. Also, Alan is getting married with her girlfriend next year, they are planning to have a baby in near future. So Alan is also curious about the welfare and #hci of children in Camden.

Scenarios

Accessibility


  • Camden’s Website has the goal to be accessible to everyone and thus offers a variety of services

    • TTS Service
    • British Sign Language Interpreting Services
    • TexMee

  • The chatbot should

    • be compatible with the Camden Council TTS service
    • be able to simplify the answer into plain english or rephrase the answer for clarification
    • have a standard font size of 12 but should also be adjustable

Evaluation & Future Development

  • With several design cycles and user feedback we were able to develop the most elegant minimal design

  • The design focuses on the conversation and heavily relies on a good chatbot

  • In the next phase a specific platform will have to be chosen for development and the design might have to be adapted accordingly

  • Another step will be adapting the design to the functionality of the bot (Does the bot need more specific queries? / How well can he/she respond?)
